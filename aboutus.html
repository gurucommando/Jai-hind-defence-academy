Eventually he was able to crack Air Force X Group and joined IAF (Indian Air Force) in 2019. His other batchmates Anshuman Tiwari, Himanshu Pandey and Abhishek Tiwari belong to Ballia district and they also joined jai hind acedemy Defence Academy with the same aim of joining Indian Air Force and wear the blue uniform.
